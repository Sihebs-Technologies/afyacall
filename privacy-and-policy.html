Privacy & Policy

We are committed to maintain the privacy of your information, and this Privacy Policy defines the practices and policies of AFYACALL HEALTH SERVICES Ltd. for handling client’s personal and medical information which may be submitted by the customer through email, telephone or face-to-face with Afyacall agents while availing our services.

A) Personal and Health Information:
  1. We may use your Personal and Health information mainly but not limited to provide Treatment, Payments & Operational purposes, and it is in order to provide efficient healthcare services remotely.
  2. The Personal and Health Information will be disclosed to the doctors employed by AFYACALL and the other doctors engaged by the company for the provision of medical health care services, consultation, follow-up treatment and for all other incidental and associated purposes relating to the provision of such services.
  3. AFYACALL will not sell or redistribute your information to anyone. We do not share your information with third parties for purposes other than that of providing you the services.
  4. Once the consultation is complete, we may share your questions (without any personal information) and respective answers by Physicians on site or social media, to enable site and page visitors to learn from your medical experiences.
B) Contact Information:
  1. Primarily your phone number may get used to provide the services offered, troubleshoot the problem, Payment notification, Deliver information to you that is relevant to your interests, such as notifications and reminders related to consultation, customize and improve your experience & to enforce our Terms & Conditions.
  2. And when you call or send an email or other communications to us, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services.
C) Public Information:
  1. Any information that you may reveal in free forum like ‘Public Question & Answer’, ‘Online Discussion’ ‘post on our social media page’, etc. is intentionally open to the public and is not in any way private, and therefore shall not be subject to this Privacy Policy.
  2. You should think carefully before disclosing any personally identifiable information in any public forum. What you have written may be seen and/or collected by third parties and may be used by others in ways which we are unable to control or predict.
D) Confidentiality and Responsibility of your Account:
  1. If you use our platform (AFYACALL) and its services, you shall be responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your PIN code and you shall be responsible for all activities that occur under your PIN code. As for a minor or an individual who needs a caretaker, the person responsible shall take full responsibility of the protection of the PIN Code in question.
  2. You are expected to be the only user of your PIN code as this will translate to the information provided and eventually form your electronic medical file.
E) Disclosure of your Personal Information without your prior consent in special cases:
  1. When the disclosure is necessary for compliance of a legal obligation
  2. Where the information is required to be shared with Government agencies as mandated under the law for the purpose of verification of identity for prevention, detection, investigation, including cyber incidents, prosecution and punishment of offences
  3. Where the information is required to be disclosed by an order under the law for the time being in force.
F) Membership:

You agree that if you provide any information that is untrue, inaccurate or incomplete or We have reasonable grounds to suspect that such information is untrue, inaccurate or incomplete, or not in accordance with the Terms & Condition, We shall have the right to indefinitely suspend or terminate or block access of your membership on the platform and refuse to provide You with access to the Websites & any other associated services.

And you are impliedly or expressly accepting and bound by these ‘Terms & Conditions’ including but not limited to ‘Privacy Policy’ available as amended from time to time.

H) Call and IP logs:
  1. The Company maintains standard call and IP logs that record data about all visitors and registered members who call the Company and/or access the website. The Company stores this information for providing services and as an analysis tool for its doctors.
