About Afyacall

WHAT IS IT

We are here to promote easy accessibility to healthcare consultation services and follow-up for all.
AFYACALL is a mobile health (mHealth) platform linking healthcare consultation consumers with licensed medical practitioners through a mobile phone both in rural and urban areas. The platform provides solution that works countrywide exploiting the great potential brought about by telecommunication network.

In Tanzania, nearly 79% of the population have got access to a mobile phone and the penetration rate is extraordinary. Despite this technological advancement but still many have no access to medical care, we have developed a solution that would promote utilization of mobile phones and promotes healthcare accessibility. The HIPAA complied system offers a secure medical history readily available by authorities that can directly be linked to certain communities and localities.
